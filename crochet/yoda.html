Yoda scarf-hood
From a failed infinity scarf to a failed Yoda hooded scarf?

I wanted to re-use the scarf, but I ended up buying more yarn because it wasn't enough and now I have 1 ball left-over...oups

Materials:
3-4 skeins of Bernat Roving Yarn colour low tide (I do not recommend: it's for spinning your own yarn, thus it will fall apart if you pull too hard)
6 mm hook, carved from a pencil
1 yarn needle
safety pins

Ressources
hood: @jayda_institches fairy tale cloak tutorial on youtube
ears: @lovinglynie crochet yoda beanie adult size tutorial on youtube

Finish: 18/02/2023
