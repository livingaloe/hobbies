Lace Bustier Upcycle
I am surprised this one turned out okay.

I bought these fast-fashion mesh tank tops years ago when layering tank tops was all the rage. It was always a struggle to style them without looking awkward!
So why not turn them into something else? I love the aesthetic of lace and I've been looking for lingerie bustiers, but I am too shy to buy them and I find them expensive.

I sewed the cups after completing most of the bustier because I was unsatisfied with the flatness.
I am glad I shortened the bottom and added a white hem a year later. I am surprised by how well this project turned out since I did not have spare fabric for mistakes.

Materials:
Lace tank top
Spaghetti strap tank top (used as reference)
White fabric (used for cup lining, boning channels, and bias tape)
A pair of satin bra straps with adjusters (do not recommend: I keep re=adjusting them because they are so slippery)
2 pairs of bras (used for cup pattern and bustier design)
Bra cups (used to pad the bustier)
Masking tape (used for pattern)
Zip ties (for boning)
Pen (for tracing and markings)
